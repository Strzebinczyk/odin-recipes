Yeast Cake

Description

Beautiful bouncy yeast cake with plenty of fruit and lemon icing.

Ingredients

  • 0.57 kg of plain flour
  • 80 g of sugar
  • 30 g of yeast
  • 250 ml of milk
  • 150 g of butter
  • 150 g of icing sugar
  • Lemon
  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1 kg of strawberries

Steps

  1. In a big bowl combine 500 g of flour with 80 g of sugar
  2. Dissolve 30 g of yeast in 250 ml of warm milk and pour into the flour mixture
  3. Wait until yeast becomes frothy and combine with 100 g of butter and 3 yolks
  4. Knead the dough until no longer sticky and leave to rest for an hour
  5. In a seperate bowl combine 50 g of butter with 70 g of flour and 50 g of icing sugar
  6. Transfer the dough to a baking tray and sprinkle the strawberries on top
  7. Crumble butter, flour and sugar mixture on top of strawberries and bake for 45 minutes in 180 degrees celsius
  8. After baking combine lemon juice with icing sugar and drizzle on top of the cake
