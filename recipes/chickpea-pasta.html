Chickpea Pasta

Description

This recipe is simple and filling vegetarian dish, perfect for a weekday dinner.

Ingredients

  • Can of cooked chickpeas
  • Dry habanero flakes
  • Spinach
  • Pasta
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Lemon

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta al dente and save some pasta water
  2. Chop up some garlic and grate lemon zest
  3. Put lemon zest, garlic and habanero flakes into the pan with olive oil and warm up until fragnant
  4. Before garlic browns too much put in the chickpeas, salt heavily and turn up the heat
  5. After few minutes put in the spinach, pasta and saved pasta water
  6. After the spinach wilts drizzle the dish with lemon juice and serve
