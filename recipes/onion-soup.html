Onion Soup

Description

With just some onion, chicken stock and bread this hearty soup will bring you warmth and comfort during cold days.

Ingredients

  • 1.5 kg of yellow onion
  • Thyme
  • Chicken stock
  • Baguette
  • Cheese
  • Garlic
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Chop the onionsand fry them with butter on low heat for about an hour until carmelised
  2. Chop up some garlic and add to the onion, fry for a minute
  3. Add chicken stock and boil for a few minutes
  4. Cut up a baguette and toast with butter, sprinkle with cheese
  5. Serve soup with cheesy baguette slices
